ABOUT US

Welcome to Slittos, where passion for gaming meets the joy of snacking! Founded with a vision to revolutionize the gaming snacking experience, Slittos is more than just a chips company—it's a lifestyle brand dedicated to fueling gaming adventures with flavor and crunch.Our journey began with a simple realization: gamers deserve snacks that are as exciting and exhilarating as the games they love to play. Tired of the same old options that failed to satisfy their cravings during intense gaming sessions,Inspired by the vibrant energy of the gaming community, we embarked on a quest to craft the perfect gaming snack. After countless hours of research, development, and taste-testing, Slittos was born—a tapioca chips that deliver a satisfying crunch and bold flavors with every bite.
